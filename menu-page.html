Our Menu

Here is a sneak peak at some of our dishes. We left a little note at the back of each image. Let your mouse over them.

Appetizer

DODO GIZZARD

Ever had a gizzard? Well, this is with a blend of plantain. carefully fried to give you the best taste ever. Unimaginable.

STICK MEAT

African meat is sumptous but what we prepared is sure to blow your mind. Well picked sticks that are chewable with the meat! Yeah! mixed with tomato, vegies and other goodies.

FRIED PLANTAIN(DODO) pronounced (DoeDoe)

The Nigerian proper meal is not complete without a spice of DODO. Well fried to leave a brown looking baby on your meal.

PLANTAIN CHIPS

Fried unripe plantain with natural oil to keep it fresh and crunchy as you munch.

Main Course

CHICKEN SUYA

Heated chicken with coal. African and Nigerian taste. Well prepared to give you an endless taste. Natural ingredients romanced to give your tongue just the perfect kiss.

SUYA

Fresh ram meat to give you the delicacy never rejected by a Nigerian. Our Hausa chef has carefully prepared this to give you the class that you deserve.

PEPPER SOUP

Cameroon pepper to give you the spicy taste you long for. Hot sauce with natural leafs to enable the dead cells in your tongue to come to life while you munch.

ISIEWU

Gotten from the Igbo word for GOAT head. Eaten from a carved wooden pot. With blends of pepper, onions and vegies. To name a few.
